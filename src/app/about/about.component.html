Seltzerly is a lifestyle and brand. It was made by and for people who think water should come from a can. It's for people who scoff at the question, Would you like still water or seltzer?. Hmmpf, SELTZER OF COURSE! It's for people who know water tastes boring, but they only want it to be slightly more interesting.

We believe water should have bubbles and taste like someone left a peel of some sort of fruit in it overnight. Like hey fruit, I can tell your there, but barely. We also believe that water shouldn't have sugar in it. We aren't hummingbirds, WE ARE SELTZERLY.
